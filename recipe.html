Featured Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

Enjoy this simple and delicious recipe for Spaghetti Carbonara...

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Mix the spaghetti with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
