Pepperoni Pizza

Description: A classic pizza topped with pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza dough
  • 200g Tomato sauce
  • 150g Mozzarella cheese
  • 100g Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the dough, then add mozzarella.
  4. Top with pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper before serving.
