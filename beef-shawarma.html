Beef Shawarma

Description: A flavorful Middle Eastern dish with tender beef.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef strips
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • Pita bread
  • Garlic sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the beef with olive oil and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the beef strips in a pan until tender and browned.
  3. Serve in pita bread with garlic sauce or toppings of your choice.
